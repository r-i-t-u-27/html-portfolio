THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO RITU

My top 3 movies of all time

JAB WE MET

The OG bollywood romcom of all time. Dreamy and entertainaing on another level. No matter how many times I watch it I will never be bored of watching it again. This movie is not just a movie,its an emotion.

SANAM TERI KASAM

A heart touching love story, people could never forget. The emotions and storyline is mindblowing with an unexpexted ending. The songs of this movie shall remain evergreen forever.

GUNJAN SAXENA

Being a fan of bollywood romcoms since forever I never thought a biopic would ever be on my favourites list but this movie compelled me to add it down. unexpeted and great work by the actress jhanvi kapoor and pankaj tripathi. This movie is not only inspiring but also relatable on another level for me. This movie has my heart.
